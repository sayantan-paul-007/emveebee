Baipan Bhaari Deva

Our debut film, "BAIPAN BHARI DEVA," has left an indelible mark on the Marathi film landscape, earning widespread acclaim both within Maharashtra and among the global Maharashtrian community. Exploring the nuances of relationships with a touch of humor, it strikes a chord with audiences.
In terms of box office success, it surpassed ₹92 crores in just 50 days, cementing its status as a sleeper hit. Notably, it holds the distinction of being the largest weekend opener in Marathi cinema and ranks as the second highest-grossing Marathi film of all time, alongside being the top-grossing Marathi film of 2023. Moreover, it set a new record for the highest single-day collection in Marathi cinema, exceeding ₹6 crores, and stands out as the most prominent female-centric film in Marathi history.
Following its triumphant theatrical run and numerous award wins, it is now streaming on Disney Hotstar.
