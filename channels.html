Dive into heart warming narratives that resonate with every Maharashtrian, transcending age, gender, dialect, and geography. As the fastest-growing social media channel in Marathi presently, we invite you to join our vibrant community and experience the essence of Maharashtra through our captivating content.

IRA

IRA is our platform dedicated to women, capturing the diverse stories that embody the multifaceted nature of femininity. It provides a space for women to share their narratives and connect with one another, offering a window into different perspectives and experiences. It fosters a collective feminine consciousness, perpetuating the timeless tradition of women uplifting and inspiring each other.
Through IRA, women continue to bond, grow, and illuminate each other's paths, keeping alive the essence of shared wisdom and empowerment.

At CONVERZE, individuals in their teens, tweens, and young adult years are welcomed to freely express themselves, exchange experiences, and develop collectively. All it requires is a conversation.

FOOD STOP

Food Stop is our dedicated social media channel focusing on all things related to food, beverages, and everything in between. From the familiar comfort of everyday meals to the exquisite delights of gourmet cuisine, and from the bustling streets to the heights of gastronomy, we embrace the entirety of the culinary world.
